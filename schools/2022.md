    <div class="section-header" data-sr>
      <span class="section-label" style="color:#4338ca">Format</span>
      <h2>A summer-school-style mini-course</h2>
    </div>
    <div data-sr>
      <p style="font-size:1.05rem; line-height:1.8; color:#444; max-width:680px;">
        Math for machine learning &mdash; probability and linear algebra &mdash; was
        taught by <strong>Raphael Flauger</strong>, while machine-learning methods
        and their applications were taught by <strong>Ndapa Nakashole</strong>.
      </p>
      <p style="font-size:1.05rem; line-height:1.8; color:#444; max-width:680px; margin-top:1.2rem;">
        Supervised learning depends on labeled data, so the course worked with
        students to collect high-quality data in <strong>Oshikwanyama</strong> for
        applications of interest, using a data annotation tool &mdash; letting
        students take ownership of the ML systems they built. The course was offered
        free of charge, with certificates on successful completion.
      </p>
    </div>
